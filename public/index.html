FAQ

Good questions, straight answers.

How long does an epoxy floor last?

With proper prep and a quality topcoat, residential floors typically last 15+ years. Commercial floors hold up 5–10 years depending on traffic.

How long will my garage be out of service?

Most garage jobs finish in 1–2 days. You can walk on the floor the next morning and drive on it 3–5 days after install.

Can you coat a cracked or oil-stained floor?

Yes. We grind the surface, repair cracks with polyurea or epoxy fillers, and de-grease oil spots before priming.

Is epoxy slippery when wet?

We add a non-slip aggregate to the topcoat so traction stays safe around vehicles, pool decks, and wash bays.

Do you offer a warranty?

Residential floors carry a lifetime warranty against peel and delamination. Commercial warranties vary by system — we spell it out on the quote.

How much does it cost?

Most 2-car garages fall between a few thousand dollars depending on prep, flake or metallic choice, and topcoat. We'll give you a firm number after a walkthrough.
