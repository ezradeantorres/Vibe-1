Common Questions

FAQ

Do you accept insurance?
We are a private pay practice. Superbills are provided so you can submit for out-of-network reimbursement with your insurance company.
What happens at the first appointment?
Your 90-minute intake covers sleep, exercise, nutrition, full medical and surgical history, preventive care, and your goals. Abbey orders extensive labs and spends hours reviewing everything before building your personalized plan.
Do you treat men too?
Absolutely. Abbey provides hormone optimization, metabolic health support, and integrative primary care for both men and women.
How is this different from a regular doctor?
Most visits are 15 minutes with basic labs. Abbey spends 60–90 minutes, orders extensive private panel testing, and creates a personalized plan based on your goals — not just your symptoms.
Where do I get labs done?
Abbey partners with local labs in the Salt Lake Valley. You'll receive lab orders and can schedule at a convenient location near you.
