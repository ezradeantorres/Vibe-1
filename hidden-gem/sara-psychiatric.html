Common Questions

FAQ

What ages do you work with?
Sara sees ages 6 and up for therapy. Medication management is considered case-by-case for those under 14.
Can I do therapy and medication management with Sara?
Yes. Sara is both a therapist and psychiatric prescriber. Having one provider for both eliminates the coordination gap that frustrates so many clients.
Does Sara work with adults?
Absolutely. Sara provides psychiatric medication management and EMDR-based therapy for adults of all ages dealing with anxiety, depression, PTSD, burnout, ADHD, and more. She is not just a children's provider.
What conditions does Sara treat?
PTSD and trauma, anxiety and social anxiety, depression, ADHD, autism spectrum, emotional regulation, life transitions, grief, relational challenges, self-esteem, and burnout. Sara also focuses on gut-brain function, inflammation, and how physical wellness impacts mental health.
Do you accept insurance?
We are a private pay practice. Insurance limits appointment length and restricts treatment options. By staying independent, we can spend the time you deserve and create truly personalized care. Superbills are provided for out-of-network reimbursement.
