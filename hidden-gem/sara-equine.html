Common Questions

FAQ

Do I need horse experience for equine therapy?
Not at all. No prior horse experience is needed. Sessions are ground-based — you won't be riding. Sara guides every interaction and the horses do the rest.
How do I choose between in-office, outdoor, or equine sessions?
You don't have to. Sessions are shaped by what you need that day. Some days call for quiet reflection indoors. Others may benefit from movement, fresh air, or the horses.
Where are equine sessions held?
Equine-assisted psychotherapy takes place at our private equine facility in Sandy, UT — by appointment only. In-office sessions are held at our Holladay location.
