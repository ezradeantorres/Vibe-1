Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you accept insurance?
We are a private pay practice. Superbills are provided so you can submit for out-of-network reimbursement with your insurance company.
What happens at a first appointment?
Intakes are 60–90 minutes and cover your full history, goals, lifestyle, and concerns. Both Sara and Abbey take the time to truly understand you before creating a personalized care plan.
What is the cancellation policy?
We require 24 hours notice for cancellation or rescheduling. One complimentary reschedule per client. After that, missed sessions are charged the full session fee.
Where are your offices?
In-office appointments and primary care are at our Holladay location (2040 E Murray Holladay Rd, Suite 102A, Holladay, UT 84117). Equine-assisted therapy takes place at our private facility in Sandy, UT — by appointment only.
Can Sara and Abbey coordinate my care?
Absolutely. With your consent, Sara and Abbey consult on shared cases. Many clients see both providers — for example, Sara for mental health and Abbey for hormone or metabolic support.
What forms of payment do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, American Express, Discover, HSA cards, and cash.
